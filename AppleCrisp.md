# Apple Crisp

Source: mom

## Ingredients

- 4 cups sliced apples
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- ½ cup uncooked quick oats
- ¼ cup flour
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- ½ tsp salt
- ⅛ tsp nutmeg

## Instructions

1. Peel and slice apples into microwave safe dish.
2. Add lemon juice.
3. Microwave apples 2.5 to 4 minutes, until softened.
4. In separate bowl, mix remaining ingredients, and then microwave for 1.5 minutes.
5. Stir the mixture, then microwave for another 1.5 minutes.
6. Pour mixture over apples, then microwave for 4 to 6 minutes.
