# Teriyaki Ground Chicken

Source: https://quickweeknightmeals.com/teriyaki-ground-beef/

## Ingredients

- 3 tsp sesame oil
- 2 lb ground chicken
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced OR 2 tsp garlic powder
- ⅓ cup soy sauce
- 2 tsp rice vinegar
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp honey
- ½ tsp ground ginger

## Optional Toppings

- sesame seeds
- sliced green onions

## Instructions

1. Heat sesame oil in a pot or frying pan over medium heat.
2. Add ground chicken, onion, and garlic.
3. Stir periodically until thoroughly cooked. Reduce heat to a simmer.
4. In a separate bowl, whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, honey, and ground ginger.
5. Pour sauce onto chicken, stir, and let simmer for about 5 minutes.
6. Add optional toppings and serve with cooked rice if desired.
