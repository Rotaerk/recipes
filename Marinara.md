# Marinara

Source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y6Bq8NrdoIk

## Ingredients

- ½ cup olive oil
- 6 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 yellow onion, minced
- 56 oz crushed tomatoes (2 28-oz cans)
- 2 tsp dried oregano
- 2 tsp dried basil
- salt
- ground black pepper
- 1 lb ground beef (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a pot or frying pan over medium heat.
2. Add the minced onion and garlic, dried oregano, 2 tsp salt, several cranks of black pepper, and ground beef.
3. Cook until onions and ground beef are done.
4. Turn heat down to low, and add crushed tomatoes to pan. Salt to taste.
5. Stir in dried basil.
